Unity game developer with 4+ years of experience building therapeutic and narrative games — from mobile apps validated in clinical research to narrative experiences shown at GDC, Play NYC, and Wonderville.

Based in Brooklyn, born and raised in India. My work sits at the intersection of game design, UX, and accessibility — I care deeply about how games make people feel, and whether those feelings can do something real in the world.

As a woman of color, representation and accessibility aren't afterthoughts — they're the starting point.
