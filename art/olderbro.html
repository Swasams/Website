About

My Older Brother is an animated GIF book adapted from Munshi Premchand's short story "My Elder Brother." The narrative follows two brothers and the pressure of academic expectation — the elder repeatedly failing while scolding the younger, who keeps passing easily.

The book format lets each spread breathe as a short looping scene — small animations on top of full illustration, designed to feel like a picture book that quietly moves.

Process

Built at home during COVID, working from a small setup. The process pages document the progression from thumbnails to inked frames to final looping GIFs.
