About

36 Days of Type is an annual challenge in which designers and animators produce one letter of the alphabet per day — 26 letters plus digits 0 through 9. For the 2021 edition I mapped each letter to a place, person, or feeling from Chennai, letting the city do the design work.

Higher-resolution versions with audio are on Instagram.
