About

Tale of Two Bulls adapts Munshi Premchand's short story of the same name. It follows two bulls who are separated, suffer under cruelty, and finally escape — a fable about friendship and the cost of freedom.

Illustrated in collaboration with StoryWeaver for their open-access children's library.
