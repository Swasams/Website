The brief

A collection of live sketches from 2018. A large portion come from the life-drawing classes I took during my exchange semester at La Cambre in Brussels — long sittings, fast poses, a room full of people drawing the same figure differently. The rest are outdoor and impromptu: people on trains, in cafes, wherever I happened to have a pen.

Nothing here is staged. It’s the most honest kind of drawing I do — quick, observational, and unembarrassed about being unfinished.
