i.

The brief

In 2018 I spent six months in Belgium on an exchange semester at La Cambre. ABCs of Belgium is what I brought back — an illustrated alphabet of the country, A to Z, each letter standing for a place, a food, a habit, or a small thing I learned to love while I was there.

It started as a way to remember the trip and turned into a portrait of it. The alphabet was a useful frame: twenty-six prompts I couldn’t talk my way out of, each one forcing me to pick the single most Belgian thing I could think of for that letter.
