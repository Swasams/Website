# Quiz Draft — "Stray Familiar"

A 3-deep narrative quiz: 1 stray cat → 4 vibes → 2 sub-themes each → 3 endings each. The cat is the throughline; she changes form to match each project's world.

Edit the prose below freely, then paste each block back into the matching passage in your Twine file. Twine link syntax (`[[link text->TargetPassage]]`) is preserved as-is so the existing graph still works.

---

## Question 1 — start

You walk out the front door and see a stray cat. She is round and cute, and as soon as she notices you, she begins to flop on her belly. What do you do?

* [[play with her->Question2A]]
* [[leave food and step back->Question2B]]
* [[follow her quietly to see where she goes->Question2C]]
* [[sit down and start sketching her in your notebook->Question2D]]

---

## Question2A — *(after: play with her)*

You pull out your keys and she paws at them. After a few minutes she seems to get bored and begins licking her paw. With a clean paw she stares at you, unblinking. 

* [[I think she's trying to tell me something. ->Question3A]]
* [[I think she just wants to keep playing.->Question3B]]

---

## Question2B — *(after: leave food and step back)*

You set down a bowl. She sniffs it, then eats small, deliberate bites, like she's been taught manners. When she's done she sits back and looks at you, head tilted. She meows softly as if to say thanks and quickly scuttles away. 

* [[Wait for her->Question3B]]
* [[She's looks like she's lived here longer than I have. She'll be fine->Question3C]]

---

## Question2C — *(after: follow her)*

She trots ahead without checking if you're behind her. You follow. She turns down a side path you've never noticed, threads through a gap in a fence, and stops at a doorway you don't recognise. She steps inside. How does the room look?

* [[ It's dark and abandoned, empty but nataure has taken over the room, growing flowers out of little cracks ->Question3C]]
* [[ One wall is covered in writing, letters, names, a kind of ledger you can't read.->Question3D]]

---

## Question2D — *(after: sketch her)*

You uncap your pen. She holds still for you, somehow knowing. You start with the curve of her back. She watches your hand move. You are looking down sketching her eyes when she suddenly she pops up behind sketchbook and places a paw on the page. 

* [[Let her add to the art->Question3D]]
* [[oop, can't have her dirty the page->Question3E]]

## Question3A — Impact games

She blinks at you, slow and deliberate. When her eyes open again they're full of tiny stars. Actual ones. She wasn't a stray. She's been looking for you, and now she wants to take you somewhere. There are three places she could lead you. Pick one.

* [[the warm one — for when the world feels too loud->StarStarter]]
* [[the strange one — for when you have something true to say but no one to say it to->Normal Bad Sex]]
* [[the sharp one — for when you want to know what's hidden->The Mix Archives]]

---

## Question3B — Board games

She's here. Tail up, paws planted, like she's been waiting for you to catch up. There are three small things on the ground beside her — a folded map, a card with rules on it, a handful of pieces. She wants to play. Pick the game.

* [[the one with running and shoving->Ram Rush]]
* [[the one with secrets and shadows->Veil]]
* [[the one where everything grows->Superbloom]]

---

## Question3C — Miscellaneous

Wherever she is now, she's at home. She always seems to be at home, in rooms that don't quite belong to her. She stops at three doorways and waits for you to look.

* [[a doorway into a house that smells of incense and quiet->Patti Veedu]]
* [[a folder of small films, each one short and a little strange->Showreel 2023]]

---

## Question3D — Type and Animations

She's interested in marks. She always has been. Letters, lines, brushstrokes. She paws at three of them in turn — but she likes one best. Pick the one she lingers on.

* [[a letter that moves — a brother's hand, a brother's voice->My Older Brother]]
* [[a letter for every day of a city you love->36 Days of type]]
* [[a letter for every place in a country you visited->ABC's of belgium]]

---

## Question3E — Books

You move the sketchbook out of reach. She huffs, clearly insulted, and pads over to a stack of paper that wasn't there a second ago. Three books. She sits on the one she wants you to open.

* [[the one with bedtime stories and bright illustrations->Illustrated story books]]
* [[the one with quiet, careful information about your body->Women's health Brochure]]
* [[the one full of strangers drawn from across many cities->Live sketches]]

# Notes

- **Q3B is reachable from both Q2A and Q2B.** Same for Q3C (from Q2B + Q2C) and Q3D (from Q2C + Q2D). The intro lines for those Q3 passages are written generically so they make sense after either path.
- **The cat's "form" is set at the ending, not earlier.** Q1–Q3 keep her as a normal stray; the morph happens at the destination. This is so the journey feels like discovery, not a costume change. Easy to push the morph earlier (e.g. into Q3) if you want.
