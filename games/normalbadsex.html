About

Normal-Bad Sex combines oral history interviews with interactive gameplay to surface awkward, funny, and hard-to-name intimate moments. Spoken stories play alongside short mini-games designed for emotional resonance rather than literal representation — characters appear as personified animals in jungle settings, preserving human feeling while abstracting the scene.

The project is built as a series, each episode pairing a new interview with new mechanics. There are no fail states; the audio auto-plays, the games are self-paced, and the player can listen and play at the same time.

My Role

  • Co-designed the interactive podcast format and game-to-audio pacing with Nyusha Impolski.
  • Built menu animations in After Effects; implemented in-engine animation in Unity.
  • Designed the connective jungle vignette — a slow-moving world that stitches together bar scenes and mini-games, with a popcorn game during bar sequences to keep the audience engaged.
  • Prototyped mini-games with one-verb actions (inspired by Wario Ware) displayed in the top-left corner at the end of each game.
