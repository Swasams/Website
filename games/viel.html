Veil

Tarot Card Storytelling Game  ·  with Tori Smith, Camron Gonzalaz, Leslie Zeng  ·  2022

A storytelling card game set in the late Victorian Era. Players gather at a Spiritualist séance, interpreting a Spirit's stories of their Past, Present, and Future — and perhaps their own as well — through custom tarot-inspired cards.

Concept

Veil asks players to sit together around a Victorian séance table and tell a story they don't fully control. One player takes the role of the Spirit; the others ask questions about its past, present, and future. The answers come from the cards.

Core Mechanic

The design hinges on a set of transparent filters that slide over each card, revealing different interpretations of the same image. When a card is drawn, the ghost player chooses which filter to apply — each filter steers the story in a different direction. The same card can become grief, or mischief, or regret depending on what's laid over it.

Visual Design

Custom tarot-inspired card art with Victorian aesthetic. The filters are printed on transparent overlays so the original card art remains visible underneath, with the filter layering new symbols and framing on top.
