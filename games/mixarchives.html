The Mix Archives

Conspiracy Game  ·  with Nyusha Impolski, Jet Vellinga, Chros Wang

An investigative narrative game about conspiracy theories and online radicalization. Browse news articles from credible and not-so-credible sources, share them with your growing following — but beware, your clicks have consequences.

About

The Mix Archives drops the player into the role of a news aggregator and community moderator. Articles come from sources with varying credibility — some legitimate, some laundering misinformation. Every share nudges your audience, and your audience is watching.

The game is designed around the slow drift of radicalization: a "You might like" rail recommends content that gradually escalates, ads become increasingly unhinged, and community sentiment shifts with your choices. Lose moderator status and the social page visibly changes underneath you.

My Role

  • Designed the news-page interface — cluttered, ad-heavy, mimicking real news-site UX while deliberately avoiding conventional social-media aesthetics.
  • Built the share and recommendation systems, including the "You might like" rail that slowly pushes the player toward fringe content.
  • Implemented the messaging / chat system with Leantween animations and a cutscene-style flow, including fast-forward.
  • Iterated on the taskbar: from a moveable iPhone-style menu to a fixed, always-visible taskbar with breaking-news quick access.
