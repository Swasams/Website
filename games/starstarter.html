Abstract

StarStarter's core therapeutic method is a game-based approach to a decades-old area of clinical and neuroscientific research known as Attention Bias Modification (ABM). Produced by Arcade Therapeutics.

My Role

As Lead Game Developer and Designer on StarStarter, I was responsible for creating and implementing core gameplay features, narrative elements, and user interface components that enhanced the game's accessibility and engagement.

  • Architected core gameplay systems, including power-ups and a refactored map with cleaner state and camera control, increasing users retained after day 1 by over 60%.
  • Implemented in-app subscriptions and promotional offer codes, streamlining App Store and Google Play release pipelines — resulting in a brand new subscription revenue stream.
  • Spearheaded onboarding design, creating a streamlined tutorial that improved user engagement, leading to a 150% increase in daily active users.
  • Mentored and managed a cross-functional team of 4 art and development interns, providing guidance on game development best practices.
  • Deployed significant improvements leading to the APA Labs Digital Badge and a 30% increase in game downloads within the initial launch period.
